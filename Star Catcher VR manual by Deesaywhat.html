🌟 What is Star Catcher VR?

Star Catcher VR is a colorful mobile VR game built with A-Frame. You explore three beautiful 3D worlds and catch glowing stars by gazing at them - no controllers needed! Avoid the grey rocks, beat the clock, and level up to become a Star Champion!

Works on any phone with a Google Cardboard VR headset, or play directly in your browser on desktop. Just look around - your gaze does all the work!

💻 How to Install and Run

Option A - Play Online (Easiest!)

  1. Open Google Chrome on your phone or computer
  2. Go to the GitHub Pages link provided in the submission
  3. The game loads instantly - no downloads needed!
  4. For VR: put your phone in a Google Cardboard headset and tap the VR button (small goggles icon in the bottom corner)

Option B - Run on Your Computer

  1. Download or unzip the project folder
  2. Open a terminal / command prompt in that folder
  3. Run: python3 -m http.server 8080
  4. Open Chrome and go to: http://localhost:8080
  5. For mobile VR on same Wi-Fi: use http://YOUR_IP:8080

🕹 How to Control the Game

VR Mode - Gaze Control (Main Method)

A small pink ring is always in the centre of your view. To catch a star:

  1. Look at a glowing star - the ring will start to grow
  2. Hold your gaze on the star for 1.2 seconds
  3. The ring fills up - then the star is caught!
  4. Look away from grey rocks - they will subtract a point!

Desktop Mode

Click Catch a star or activate any button
Mouse drag Look around the 3D world
Esc Exit VR mode

Mobile (Non-VR)

Tap Catch stars and press buttons
Drag Look around
Tilt Phone Move your view (gyroscope)

🌍 The Three Worlds

🌸 Flower Meadow

A bright daytime field with soft green hills, colourful trees and drifting white clouds. Stars and collectibles float gently through the air above the flowers. The warm sunlight creates a cheerful, relaxing atmosphere.

🚀 Outer Space

A dark galaxy with hundreds of twinkling stars in the background, giant ringed planets, a glowing moon and a cute pink rocket. Stars here glow even brighter against the night sky, making them easier to spot - but they move a little faster!

🐠 Under the Sea

A colourful underwater world with sandy patches, tall coral in pink, teal and yellow, swimming fish and drifting bubbles. The blue lighting and gentle glow make for a magical and calm atmosphere while you catch the glowing stars underwater.

🎮 How to Play

  1. At the Flash Screen, wait for the game to load (about 5 seconds)
  2. On the Setup Screen, choose your settings and press PLAY!
  3. On the World Menu, gaze at one of the three world buttons to choose it
  4. A countdown (3... 2... 1... GO!) will appear before the round starts
  5. Stars will float into the world - gaze at them to catch them!
  6. Avoid grey rocks - gazing at them takes away a point
  7. The timer counts down - catch as many stars as you can before time runs out!
  8. After the round, see your final score and choose to play again!

⭐ Level System

As you catch more stars your level increases! Higher levels mean stars appear faster for more challenge.

LevelStars NeededWhat Changes
Level 1 - Beginner0 starsNormal speed, easy to catch
Level 2 - Star Finder10 starsStars appear a little faster
Level 3 - Star Hunter25 starsEven faster - stay focused!
Level 4 - Star Hero45 starsVery fast - rock dodging matters now
Level 5 - Star Champion70 starsMaximum speed - true champion!

A big colourful "Level Up!" banner appears every time you advance. Keep going!
